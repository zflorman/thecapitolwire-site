H.R. 2294 — To reauthorize the Integrated Coastal and Ocean Observation System Act of 2009., as amended

Current Status
Introduced in House

Executive Summary

H.R. 2294 seeks to reauthorize the Integrated Coastal and Ocean Observation System Act of 2009. This reauthorization aims to enhance data sharing and collaboration among regional and federal entities involved in coastal and ocean observation. The bill modifies the structure of the observation system, updates operational procedures, and authorizes specific funding levels for the coming years. Passage would ensure continued operation of a critical data collection and dissemination program.
