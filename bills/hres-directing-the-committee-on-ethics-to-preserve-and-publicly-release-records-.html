Executive Summary

This resolution directs the House Committee on Ethics to preserve and publicly release records related to violations of clause 9 (sexual harassment) and clause 18 of rule XXIII. The resolution aims to increase transparency and accountability regarding sexual harassment within the House of Representatives. It addresses concerns about unwelcome sexual advances and discrimination. The resolution seeks to ensure the safety and dignity of individuals within the House.
