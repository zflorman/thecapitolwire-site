H.R. 5764 — AI for Main Street Act, as amended

Current Status
Reported with an amendment, committed to the Committee of the Whole House on the State of the Union, and ordered to be printed

Executive Summary

H.R. 5764 amends the Small Business Act to mandate that small business development centers (SBDCs) assist small businesses in utilizing artificial intelligence (AI). This bill aims to modernize SBDC services and ensure small businesses can leverage AI for growth and competitiveness. The bill was reported with an amendment and committed to the Committee of the Whole House on the State of the Union, signaling progress in the legislative process. This bill addresses the growing need for small businesses to adopt AI technologies to remain competitive.

Arguments Against

  • Argument 1: Opponents may argue that the bill creates an unfunded mandate for SBDCs, straining their existing resources.
  • Argument 2: Concerns may arise regarding the potential for misuse or bias in AI technologies promoted by SBDCs.
  • Argument 3: Implementation challenges may include a lack of qualified AI experts within SBDCs and difficulties in tailoring AI solutions to diverse small business needs.

📋 Key Provisions

  • Requires SBDCs to assist small businesses in evaluating AI concerning operations.
  • Mandates SBDCs to provide information, guidance, and training on the use of AI.
  • Amends Section 3 of the Small Business Act to define 'artificial intelligence' by referencing Section 5002 of the National Artificial Intelligence Initiative Act of 2020.
