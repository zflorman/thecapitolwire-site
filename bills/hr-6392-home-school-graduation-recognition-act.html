H.R. 6392 — Home School Graduation Recognition Act, as amended

Current Status
Reported with an amendment

Executive Summary

H.R. 6392 amends the Higher Education Act of 1965 to recognize homeschooled students as high school graduates for the purposes of college admission and federal financial aid. This change addresses concerns that current regulations may disadvantage homeschooled students. The bill has been reported with an amendment and is ready for consideration by the full House.

📋 Key Provisions

  • Amends Section 484(d) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 to include homeschooled students.
  • Ensures that homeschooled students who complete secondary education in a setting treated as a home school or private school under State law are considered high school graduates.
