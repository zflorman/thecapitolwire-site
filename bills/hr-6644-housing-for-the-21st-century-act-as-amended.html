Current Status
Introduced

Executive Summary

H.R. 6644, the Housing for the 21st Century Act, aims to increase the supply of housing in America by modernizing local development, expanding affordable housing finance opportunities, and enhancing oversight of housing providers. Introduced in the House of Representatives, the bill addresses concerns about housing affordability and availability by streamlining regulations, incentivizing new construction, and supporting community banks' role in housing finance. The bill seeks to update existing programs and foster innovation in housing markets.
