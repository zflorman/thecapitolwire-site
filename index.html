House Floor Watch

House Floor Watch is a free, opt-in email alert service that monitors the U.S. House of Representatives’ official “Bills This Week” page and sends an email only when the House posts an official update.

Subscribe Contact

What it monitors

House Floor Watch monitors the House’s official “Bills This Week” posting. The House documentation site is treated as the single source of truth.

This service does not predict floor action, interpret House communications, or provide analysis. It only alerts subscribers when the official schedule page changes.

When emails are sent

  • No speculation, analysis, or commentary
  • No duplicate alerts
  • Often zero emails in a day
  • One email per official House update

Who it’s for

House Floor Watch is intended for congressional staff, advocates, policy professionals, and others who rely on timely, official schedule updates.

Unsubscribe and privacy

You may unsubscribe at any time using the instructions included in every alert email. House Floor Watch does not sell subscriber information and does not use website tracking or analytics.

House Floor Watch is an independent informational service and is not an official U.S. House of Representatives resource.
