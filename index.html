House Floor Watch

Official U.S. House schedule alerts. No spam, no speculation.

How it works

We monitor the official Bills This Week page. When the House Clerk posts an update, our system detects the change and emails you immediately.

Our Promise

  • Zero speculation: We only send what is officially posted.
  • Zero clutter: If the schedule doesn't change, you don't get an email.
  • Privacy first: We never sell your email or track your clicks.

Who is this for?

Designed for congressional staff, policy professionals, and advocates who need to know exactly when bills are added to the floor schedule.

You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email.
