Why Hill Staff Use This Tool

The official House Clerk website is the gold standard for data, but it lacks a notification system. We bridge that gap.

Faster than Refreshing

We ping the official server every minute. You'll often know about a schedule change before the PDF even finishes downloading on your colleague's computer.

Zero Noise Policy

No opinion pieces. No newsletters. No fundraising. You ONLY get an email when the official House Floor Schedule (`Default.aspx`) is updated.

Change Detection

We don't just send you the new link. Our bot compares the old schedule to the new one and highlights exactly what changed (e.g., "Added H.R. 5457").

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this affiliated with the House?
No. This is an independent tool built to help staff monitor public data more efficiently. We simply check docs.house.gov so you don't have to.
How fast are the alerts?
Our system checks for updates every 60 seconds. Notifications usually land in your inbox within 2-3 minutes of the Clerk posting the update.
Does it cost anything?
No. It is currently free for all House staff and government affairs professionals.
