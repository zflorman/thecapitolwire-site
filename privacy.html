Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

The Capitol Wire ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect your email address when you voluntarily subscribe to our alerts. We do not collect names, phone numbers, or IP addresses.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your email is used solely to send you notifications regarding the U.S. House Floor Schedule. We do not sell or rent your email address to third parties.

3. Data Security

We use secure, industry-standard database services (Supabase) to store your information. We do not store personal data on local devices.

4. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future. If we make significant changes to how we handle your data, we will notify you via email.

5. Contact

For questions, contact us at contact@thecapitolwire.com.
